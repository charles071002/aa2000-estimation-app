# Consumables — Market Research: Mean Average Pricing (Philippines)

This document presents **mean average prices per piece** (or per single unit: tube, roll, can) in Philippine Peso (₱) for consumables used in fire protection, security, and electrical installations. Where items are sold in boxes or packs, the price is converted to **per piece** using typical pack sizes (e.g. self-tapping screws ₱4/pc from a box of ~₱85). Prices are based on Philippine retail and B2B sources (Ace Hardware PH, RS Online Philippines, Galleon.ph, Lazada PH, Electx Electrical Supply, Hilti Philippines, ToolsSavvy.ph). Values are suitable for BOQ and cost computation when quantity is entered in **pieces**.

## 6. Notes for Cost Computation

- **Per piece:** Fastening items (screws, washers, nuts & bolts, etc.) use **per-piece** prices derived from box/pack prices (e.g. self-tapping screws ₱4/pc from a box of ~₱85). Enter quantity in **pieces**; cost = quantity × ₱/piece.
- **Other items:** Sealants, tapes, markers, etc. are priced per single unit (1 tube, 1 roll, 1 pack). Quantity = number of those units.
- **Consumables Total:** Sum of (quantity × ₱/piece or ₱/unit) for each line.
- **VAT:** Add 12% VAT to subtotal when required for billable amounts.
- **Sourcing:** Prices reflect NCR and major retail/B2B channels; regional and bulk discounts will vary.
- **Updates:** Revise periodically; last compiled from 2024–2025 data.

*Sources: Ace Hardware PH, RS Online Philippines, Galleon.ph, Lazada PH, Electx Electrical Supply, Hilti Philippines, ToolsSavvy.ph, and industry benchmarks.*
